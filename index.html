What’s your current skill level?

Your current skill level helps determine your ideal shape and style.

We recommend getting an “All Mountain” snowboard at this skill level.

With an All Mountain board, you can explore different styles of riding while you continue to improve your skills.

What’s your preferred riding style?

Freeride boards are for carving groomed runs or surfing powder. Freestyle boards are for the terrain park and hitting jumps. All Mountain boards can handle any part of the mountain.

What kind of freestyle riding do you prefer?

A park board will be shorter and softer with less camber than a board for hitting jumps.

What type of freeriding do you prefer?

A powder board will help you stay afloat, a carving board will have the best edge hold.

What's more important to you when riding powder?

A rocker or combination camber will help you float, a regular camber provides more control.
